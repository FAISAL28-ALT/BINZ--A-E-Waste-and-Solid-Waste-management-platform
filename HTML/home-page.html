About us
Goals and Objectives
Our mission is to simplify and revolutionize scrap collection by making it accessible, transparent, and rewarding. Unlike large-scale companies, we focus on local communities, residential areas, and college campuses, ensuring a seamless waste management solution. Through our tech-driven approach, users can easily schedule pickups via a website, app, or WhatsApp, ensuring convenience and fair pricing. We aim to empower local scrap collectors by partnering with them directly, eliminating middlemen, and providing better wages and work opportunities. Additionally, our system introduces reward-based incentives, allowing users to earn discounts or cashback for responsible recycling. By making waste disposal beneficial for both individuals and the environment, we encourage sustainable habits and greater community participation. Starting small and scalable, our model ensures hyper-local impact while laying the groundwork for future expansion. In the long run, we envision integrating AI-driven waste sorting and recycling tracking to optimize the process.
